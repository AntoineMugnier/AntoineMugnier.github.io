Hello, I'm Antoine

I'm an embedded software engineer with 4 years of experience developing firmware for microcontrollers. Having initially a background in biotechnology, I reoriented toward embedded systems just at the end of this degree and performed a M2 in Embedded Intelligence at the Univsersity of CY Cergy in apprenticeship during one year at a biotech startup called Depixus. Then I was enrolled as a firmware developer and worked for 3 years leading the development of the firmware of the future commercial instrument of the company. Here are some of my skills:

  • Proficiency with Rust, C++ and C
  • Experience working with STM32H7/STM32F4/STM32F1 (Cortex-M) and ESP32 microcontrollers
  • Experience developing all layers of the firmware, from the architecture to the drivers
  • Adept of the SCRUM methodology and Devops
